About Us

Ganesh Valliappan, Ayush Ajain, Mat Li, and Daniel Zheng were motivated to optimize note-taking and create an interactive user experience. Traditional note-taking often leads to a one-way communication between textbooks and students because most students brainlessly take notes and usually do not have a way to enforce their learning through small quizzes or summaries right after their reading. A couple of studies in New York show that students retain textbook knowledge most efficiently by reading summaries or taking small quizzes right after finishing their textbook readings. Therefore, Ganesh, Ayush, Mat, and Daniel decided to create a web application that will provide these things! They revolutionized the website by allowing users to take pictures of their reading instead of forcing users to type up their reading (which will turn it into a brainless chore rather than an interactive activity). Furthermore, they specifically wanted to create a web application because, according to a few international studies, many students around the world do not have access to smartphones, but a lot more have access to computers. The creators also made the website mobile-friendly to allow smartphone users easy access to this brilliant application. Therefore, students, or anyone else who wants to learn through textbook lessons, do not hesitate to create a free account on Scribe to start your interactive reading experience today!
